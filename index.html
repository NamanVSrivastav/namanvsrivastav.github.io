About Me

I am Naman V Srivastav, a recent Computer Science graduate specializing in AI and ML from PSIT, Kanpur. I’m passionate about developing impactful AI models to drive innovation in real-world applications. Throughout my academic journey, I engaged in rigorous research across areas like federated learning, quantum machine learning, and NLP, deepening my expertise and commitment to the field.


One of my key projects is a cardiovascular risk prediction system leveraging Federated Learning and Feedforward Neural Networks. This model, which achieved 92% accuracy, enhances patient engagement in cardiovascular health management by 30% while ensuring data privacy. My research on this work was published in the International Conference on Innovations in Computing (ICRIC-2023), Volume 2.


Additionally, I developed VIBE (Virtual Intelligence for Business Enhancement), an LSTM-based stock prediction model with 87% accuracy, and Project Opinify, a customer sentiment analysis tool for e-commerce. Opinify uses Flask for backend ML processing and Streamlit for a visually appealing, user-friendly interface, creating an accessible platform for actionable sentiment insights.
